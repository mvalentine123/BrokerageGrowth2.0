# Brokerage-in-a-Box: How We Scaled to 100 Agents in 5 Months

The recruiting systems and growth infrastructure behind it—what we actually did, what we installed, and what you can reuse.

## Why “Brokerage-in-a-Box”?

Most brokerages grow by personality and hustle. We wanted systems: clear playbooks, the right tech, and repeatable processes so that growth didn’t depend on one person. That’s what we mean by “brokerage-in-a-box”—the core pieces you need to recruit, onboard, and support agents at scale.

## Recruiting Systems That Actually Fill the Pipeline

We stopped relying on job boards and one-off referrals. We put in place a recruiting system: defined roles, consistent outreach, and a simple scorecard so we could compare candidates quickly. We also made the application and first touchpoints smooth so serious agents didn’t drop off before the first call.

## Onboarding That Sticks

Getting to 100 agents in 5 months only works if new agents get productive fast. We built a structured onboarding path—training, tools access, and clear first 30/60/90 goals—so everyone knew what “done” looked like and we could spot who needed help early.

## The Infrastructure Behind Growth

Growth doesn’t scale without infrastructure: CRM, transaction management, marketing and lead tools, and clear reporting. We chose a small set of tools and wired them together so the team could focus on agents and deals instead of duct-taping systems.

## What You Can Do Next

You don’t have to copy our exact stack. You do need the same kinds of systems: recruiting playbooks, onboarding that sticks, and infrastructure that supports scale. We’re sharing the frameworks and checklists we used so you can install your own brokerage-in-a-box.
